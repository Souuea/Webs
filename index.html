Interest in IT

My interest in IT stems from attending a UTS work experience course when I was 14, where I was shown how important IT security is additionally one of the lecturers demonstrated his attempt to hack through a company’s secure firewall and try to steal critical information, after his hack is complete he then goes back and explains to them how he achieved it so they can better their own security. He also helps construct systems and firewalls that are nearly impenetrable.

To me IT is the future, I believe there will be an almost endless amount of career opportunities if you are within the IT industry. Personally, I would like to have a career in cyber security as I love problem-solving and challenging myself.

After looking at all the courses available within each university I concluded that the course at RMIT “Introduction to Information Technology” was perfect. I am hoping that during or after my studies I will be able to pursue a more defined path within IT, after all, I would like to find a job in cyber security but I may find other areas of interest after familiarizing myself with more of the industry.
