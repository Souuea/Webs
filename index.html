Project Idea

Overview

For my IT project idea, I intend to create a game sharing product that enables you to share your games with your friends and family. This Product would include elements such as a website, online advertising, downloadable software, and gaming accessories. The aim and purpose of this Product are to be able to share paid games without each person having to pay for the game. The target audience for this gaming app is teenagers to young adults.

Motivation

I don’t necessarily have any Motivation that made me want to produce this product, but I have always wanted to be able to share the games I have paid for. Unfortunately, there is no platform that can store all games from multiple companies, my goal is to make it possible to store and share games from multiple platforms.

Description

The purpose of this Product is to be able to share paid games without each person having to pay for the game. I would like to have this product available for everyone who would like it, in the future I would think of evolving it from downloadable software to a sellable hard drive. At this time once you have downloaded the software you will be given the option to create an account after you have done that you will be given the option to join a party or create one, keep in mind you can only create or join one party. When that is done you will be given the option to a free version of the product which holds up to 1 game and can be shared between 2 people, you will also be given a 30 day trial for the full version, this enables you to be able to store up to 30 games and share it between 20 friends, after the 30 days it will cost $50, also keep in mind you will only be able to share your games, and only other people with the full version can access more than 1 game, along with the paid version you can order an additional little hard drive that enables faster and more storage for your games.

Availability To Product

I will be creating a website where you will be able to download the app and software needed. There is also an offline option for the full version that enables you to download offline games and be able to play them offline. This website will also have a list of all the parties you will be able to join (unless you are creating one) this means you can find a party with all the games you want and apply to join, the leader of the party has the power and can either accept you or not. Also on the website, there will be all the information you need and a 24hr communications service if you are having any troubles.

When / if you purchase the full version you will see that you can also purchase a hard drive, this will enable you to be able to run the games you play faster and also can store the games on in if you want to free up some space on your device, tho it will be an additional $40.
